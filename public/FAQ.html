Food Waste

Annually, millions of dollars of food is wasted and left to rot in garbage dumps. Food waste effects us all, natural resources required to grow the food and green house gases that are generated by decomposing food have long lasting evironmental damage.

Effectively, when you throw away food is is also throwing away money. Inorder to understand our food waste impact, Waste Hub can help track all of this!

What is WasteHub?

Waste Hub is a mobile web app that helps track your food waste. Whenever you have wasted food, you can log the item wasted and every Week a report is generated that will indicate how much of a certain food you have thrown away. With this information you can change what items you shop for and save yourself a bit of money.

You can also Challenge fellow WasteHub users on our weekly leader boards. See who's improved the most! Wasted the most!

Account Creation

Getting Started is simple!

If you have a google account, select login with your google acount, otherwise you can create an account with a prefered e-mail.

Your Waste Dashboard

From your waste Dashboard you will be able to access all elements of your WasteHub account. The features including:

  • Adding Waste into your weekly bin
  • Check Past Waste reports
  • Check WasteHub Leaderboards
Waste Tracking

When you have logged in, Whenever you throw away a food item, log it down. The simplest way:

  1. Login to your account
  2. Select the Add waste button
  3. Select a food group icon
  4. Select the food item icon to put one unit into your virtual waste bin

Your Dashboard will graphically show you the waste you have accumulated over the current week.

Waste Reports

Weekly waste tracking will be availble on your dashboard. For monthly or yearly metrics you can track it on your Waste Reports overlay by tapping on the left cheveron.

Reminders

Need motivation? A friendly reminder can be given to you to check your waste bin!

Challenge Friends!

Tag and remind your friends. Compete on the leader boards to see who can reduce their waste the best!
