WasteHub

WasteHub is the solution to one of the biggest issues today: food waste. In Metro Vancouver alone, nearly $700 million worth of food is wasted every year and about a third of that comes from consumers. Food Vault can helps to tackle this issue by allowing you to track every biodegradable product in your fridge. It's limited to fruits and vegetables only because those products make up a large number of wasted produce.


Food Vault is a project by five British Columbia Institute of Technology students, as part of the Computer Systems Technlogy program. This project is in part with the City of Metro Vancouver to help spread awarness regarding food waste.
